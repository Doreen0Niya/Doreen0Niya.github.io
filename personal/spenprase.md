## Research Interests

My research is focused on interactions between glaciers and rivers and understanding how changes in glaciation impacts landscape provesses. My research uses a wide range of tools including remote sensing, geochronological methods, and (my favorite) lots of fieldwork.

## Research Areas
Fluvial geomorphology, fluvial and glacial systems, geochronology, remote sensing
